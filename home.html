About Us

The Jyothinagar Arya Vysya Abhyudaya Sangam (JAVAS) is a dedicated non-profit organization based in Karimnagar. We are committed to the social, cultural, and economic upliftment of our community.

Following the divine principles of Sri Kanyaka Parameswari (Vasavi Matha) - Dharmam (Righteousness), Seelam (Integrity), and Ahimsa (Non-violence) - our Sangam actively organizes philanthropic activities to foster unity and progress among our members.
