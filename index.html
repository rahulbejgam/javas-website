About Us

Jyothi Nagar, located in the heart of Karimnagar city, stands as a distinctive and prominent locality. Nearly 260 Arya Vysya families reside in this area, making it a well-developed and socially strong community. Once known for its unique identity in Karimnagar, Jyothi Nagar continues to maintain its prominence even today. Almost every household has a software engineer, and many residents are recognized business owners in various fields. With a blend of entrepreneurs and professionals, Jyothi Nagar has emerged as one of the most prestigious and expensive residential areas in Karimnagar. About two years ago, inspired by the vision of a few like-minded individuals, the Jyothi Nagar Arya Vysya Abhyudaya Sangham (JAVAS) was formed. The main objective behind establishing this association was to strengthen unity within the community. Through various programs conducted under the leadership of this Sangham, women, youth, and elders of Jyothi Nagar are brought together, fostering a warm and friendly environment. Earlier, many families were not well acquainted with each other, but now, through these activities, bonds of affection, mutual respect, and togetherness have significantly grown among the Arya Vysya families of Jyothi Nagar.
